Who are we? TruBe is the UK's first fitness on-demand app, we are on a mission to make fitness convenient and offer a smart solution for trainers and clients to live an active life.

Download our app and book a trainer to your preferred location; in your house, at a park, in your office or at one of our new studios. Our iPhone app connects customers with fitness professionals making fitness accessible and affordable. Launched at the start of 2016, the TruBe community is growing daily.
