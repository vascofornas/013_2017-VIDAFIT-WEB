Personal Training

The most efficient way to get in shape and improve your fitness with industry leading fitness professionals. Our signature personal training sessions focus on mobility, burning fat, muscle gain and help you reach your fitness goals faster.

Why should I do it?

To learn a new way of working out, add some variety to your workout, train in a way that is specific to your needs or even if you just want to have fun.

Who is it good for?

Book a personal training session if you want to:

  • Lose fat, tone, increase fitness levels and up your strength
  • Increase your fitness, speed and agility for sports such as football, tennis, hockey, and rugby
  • Increase muscle size when combined with the right diet
  • Get ready for an event such as an obstacle course or a 10k run
  • Improve coordination and skill

Yoga

We focus on the 4th limb of Yoga which is the "quietening of the mind". We realise that without Pranayama there is no Yoga, no matter how advanced your postures are, therefore each movement emphasises the breathing and flow of the movement, focusing on your mind and body in one.

Why should I do it?

In our busy culture with the need for more and more, busy busy busy, we create stress, this is the number one killer, with a good all round practice of Yin and Yang you will soon learn to bring yourself back to the core and to the true meaning of your practice.

Who is it good for?

Book a Yoga session if you want to:

  • Decrease stress levels and improve your mood
  • Improve muscle tone
  • Improve your posture
  • Improve your core strength

Boxing / Kickboxing

Boxing and kickboxing are combat sports which require good levels of skill, co-ordination, cardio and power. We offer training sessions for up to three people that will teach you the fundamentals of the sports or improve on your current form.

Why should I do it?

Whether you're looking to start a new sport, improve on your current skills or reduce stress, our boxing or kickboxing sessions improve your mobility, motor skills and coordination. And most importantly, they're fun!

Who is it good for?

Book a boxing or kickboxing session if you are:

  • Looking to start a new sport
  • Looking to improve your current skills
  • Looking to try something new

Ballet Fit

A ballet-inspired workout for lean muscles and improved balance. This workout combines small, isolated movements with lots of reps to burn calories and sculpt muscles.

Why should I do it?

Fun but demanding, this workout uses principles of classical ballet, floor work and strength exercises to improve core stability. Ballet Barre is a great way to develop lean muscle mass while improving your balance, flexibility, coordination and mobility. It also builds strength and endurance.

Who is it good for?

Book a Ballet Barre workout if you:

  • Enjoy ballet or dance classes
  • Want a long and lean body, like a ballet dancer
  • Want to improve posture and flexibility
  • Want to build your endurance levels

Pilates

The complete strengthening of your body through mat based exercises. Pilates' slow and controlled movements puts minimal impact on your joints and will definitely tone up your muscles - particularly your core!

Why should I do it?

Pilates incorporates modern exercise science and rehabilitation principles, it is a safe and highly effective low-impact way to stretch, strengthen and streamline the body and helps to develop optimal strength, flexibility, endurance and posture, without building bulk or stressing joints, which makes it the perfect complement to cardiovascular exercise, athletic training or rehabilitation.

Who is it good for?

Book a Pilates workout if you:

  • Improve your coordination, core strength and mobility
  • Improve your posture and muscular balance
  • Develop well toned and lean muscles
  • Improve your flexibility and develop endurance
