FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

General

What is TruBe?

TruBe is a flexible, on-demand approach to personal training. Whether you want to work out at home, in the local park or at one of our exclusive venues, our app connects you with top trainers of your choice that are available for a session at a time and place that suits you.

Why are you called TruBe?

The name comes from 'True' and 'Being', because when it's easier to stay motivated, live well and feel great, you can be the truest version of you.

What services does TruBe offer?

With TruBe you can get a personal trainer at a time and a place that fits in with your schedule. Our personal trainers are equipped to help you to achieve a wide range of goals. Whether you are looking to lose weight, get stronger or to get fitter our experts can help you. We offer a variety of services including, yoga, boxing, pilates, kickboxing and ballet fit.

How old do I have to be to use TruBe?

You will need to be 18 or older to have a TruBe account.

How do I download the app?

You can download the TruBe app from the iTunes App Store. TruBe is iOS-only for now but we're working on an Android version.

Booking Sessions

When can I work out?

Sessions can be booked seven days a week between 6 am and 10 pm. You can book up to one week in advance or with only an hour and 15 minutes' notice. You can even have multiple sessions a day!

Where can I workout?

You choose the location for your workout. Whether this is in a local park, at your home or at one of our exclusive venues, our trainers only need the size of a yoga mat for your workout.

How do I register?

Download from the App Store and then register using your Facebook account or an email address. We also allow you to have guest accounts but recommend that you setup an account to be able to track your progress.

How do I login?

After registering, you can login using your Facebook account or email address.

Can I use any password?

Your password must be at least 8 characters long and include an uppercase letter and a number. Don't worry if you forget your password, you can always reset it.

How do I make my first booking?

You'll need to send a session request for a relevant and available trainer to accept. Follow these simple steps:

  1. Login to the app and select the type of session, the time and date.
  2. Choose to workout in with one of our trainers in an exclusive venue or request a trainer to come to your location.
  3. A TruBe Trainer has an hour to accept your request or suggest a slightly different time.
  4. You can then choose your trainer from the list. Your preferred trainers will be at the top of the list. We also encourage you to browse through "Our Trainers" before making a request.
  5. Trainers can also suggest alternative slots in case your prefered trainer is not available at your exact time.
  6. Once you are happy with your chosen trainer, tap the time that you would like to workout and you will be taken to the session details page.
  7. Scan your credit card or enter your payment details manually (you can choose to save these for next time). Payment will be taken only once your session is complete.
  8. You must enter your mobile number so that our trainer can contact you if he has any problems reaching you.
  9. Here you can also select the number of people who will be taking part in the workout and you can leave any additional information or directions you think your trainer will find helpful in the notes section.
  10. Tap 'Send to Trainers'.
  11. After your session, we'll email a receipt and prompt you to leave a rating for your trainer.

Can I request a particular trainer?

As long as they are available, you can decide who to train with before you book your session. You can favourite any of our trainers to receive your request first. If they are available at your chosen time they will appear at the top of the list.

How do I favourite a trainer?

You can favourite any of our trainers, which can then be found in the burger menu.

What happens if there are no trainers are available?

We work with a wide network of trainers so it's unlikely your request will ever be declined. However, there may be rare occasions during high-demand periods when we won't be able to find a free trainer. In which case, you won't be charged and you can send a new request for a different time.

How can I change the time and date of my booking?

You can modify your booking using the 'Modify Session' button, as long if you're within the cancellation window (12 hours before your session). You can also cancel the booking before this time and request a new session.

Pricing and payments

How much does a session cost?

There's no contract or monthly fee, prices start from £50 (including VAT) per one-off session. The price remains the same if you decide to invite up to two friends.

We also offer a monthly subscription plan, called TruPlan. You can choose from:

  • 4 sessions from £140 per month
  • 8 sessions from £272 per month
  • 12 sessions from £396 per month
  • 16 sessions from £512 per month

If you choose to workout in one of our exclusive venues there may be an additional cost for your workout. This additional cost will be shown on the venue booking page and is paid to the venue on arrival.

What if I run out of sessions on my TruPlan?

Don't worry! If you use up all of your sessions, you can purchase top-up sessions at £35 per session. We also have a referral scheme in place where you can earn free sessions when you refer a friend to sign-up to a TruPlan.

What is Trainer Surcharge?

Our most experienced or in demand trainers may apply an extra charge to your booking if you choose to book with them.

If you have purchased a TruPlan and you have chosen a trainer who has applied a surcharge, you will incur an extra cost. This extra cost will be taken from the payment method added to your account, once the session is completed or if the session is cancelled within 12 hours of it starting.

How do I pay for a session?

We'll ask for the details of your preferred credit card after you've sent your first session request. We charge your card after you've completed your session and send a receipt by email.

If you have subscribed to a TruPlan we will take payment as soon as you subscribe and then monthly, unless cancelled.

Are my in-app payments safe?

You can feel confident making payments with the TruBe app, which meets the relevant payment regulations and security protocols. Please see our Privacy Policy for full details.

Can I share my plan with my friends and use them individually or as a group?

The short answer to this is yes, as long as everyone books their sessions with the same account that the plan was purchased on. If these are individual sessions, just specify in the Notes section who the session is for. We've planned a more elegant way of sharing plans as part of our product roadmap, so watch this space!

Can I gift a session?

You can purchase a gift card for 4 or 8 sessions from our website. The card will be shipped to your UK address. Simply enter this on checkout.

TruPlans

What is a TruPlan?

A TruPlan is a monthly subscription of 4, 8 and 12 sessions. You will have access to all of our services and all of our highly-rated trainers at a reduced rate. The number of sessions are valid for a month from the date you first purchased your plan.

How do I subscribe to a TruPlan?

To purchase a TruPlan you must be a registered TruBe user. You can subscribe using the 'Buy TruPlan' button in the main menu.

You can also buy a TruPlan instead of purchasing a 'pay-as-you-go' session before you book a session.

Does my TruPlan renew automatically?

Yes, your TruPlan will renew automatically every month unless you cancel your plan. We will take payment on the same date every month where possible.

How do I upgrade or downgrade my plan?

You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at anytime. Your upgrade or downgrade will only occur after your next billing date. To do this press the my TruPlan button in the burger navigation tab and then the 'Change my plan' button.

Can I pause my TruPlan?

If you need a break from your sessions, you can pause your TruPlan at no extra cost for as long as you need. We won’t take any more monthly payments until you resume your plan.

When you're ready to resume your TruPlan, any previously unused sessions will still be available, and your subscription will pick up from where it left off.

You are still able to complete any sessions already booked.

How do I purchase top-up sessions?

If you have used up all of your sessions for the month you can purchase top-up sessions at a discounted price. The discount will be applied automatically so just a book a session as normal and we will take payment at the end of your session.

How do I cancel my TruPlan?

You can cancel your plan at anytime. Once cancelled, your plan will remain active for the current month, giving you a chance to finish off any sessions. On your next billing date, we will no longer take payments. To do this tap the My TruPlan button in the app and then tap on the 'Cancel my plan' button.

Can I still use a promo code with my TruPlan?

Yes, you can. When you use promo code, the session will not be deducted from your plan. If there is still an outstanding amount to be paid after having applied the promo code, we will charge you at the end of your session.

If you are using a promo code, the discount will be applied to the total cost of your booking including any additional charges from the trainer. For example, if you have a 50% off promo code and are purchasing a pay-as-you go session for £50, and you picked a trainer who has added a surcharge of £10, the discount will be applied to the full amount of £60, reducing your session to £30.

If the promo code exceeds the value of the booking the session will be no cost.

Referral codes

Do you have a referral scheme?

Yes, we do! If you have an active TruPlan and refer a friend to TruBe, you will both each get a free session credited to your account when they sign-up for their first TruPlan.

How long do the free sessions last for?

Free sessions do not expire and are carried over automatically every month. You can then use your free session once all the sessions in your current plan have been used up.

How do I refer my friends?

You can share your Referral Code using the app by going into the Free Sessions section and tapping on Share My Code. You can then post your Referral Code to your social media channels, email or text message.

How do my friends use my Referral Code?

Your friends can simply enter your Referral Code on the screen when they are purchasing their TruPlan.

I'm getting an error message telling me that a particular Referral Code is incorrect or cannot be used

An Referral Code can only be used if the person using it has not had a TruPlan before. Also, please check that the code entered is correct.

Cancellation policy

What is your cancellation policy?

You'll be charged for your session if cancel within 12 hours of your session starting and you have confirmed your booking with a trainer. For same-day bookings, you'll be given a 5-minute window to make a cancellation free of charge. If your session was part of a TruPlan, this will be deducted from your plan. We'll let you know if any cancellation fees apply before you cancel your session.

How do I cancel a TruBe session?

Go to the date of your booking in the app to find the session you want to cancel. Scroll down and tap 'Cancel Session' at the bottom of your screen.

How do I split or share payment of a session?

Whilst you can train with up to two friends, we haven't currently got the functionality to split payments in-app.

Our trainers

What qualifications do TruBe trainers have?

All our trainers have a minimum of Level 3 REPs and have passed the TruBe qualification assessment.

How do you screen trainers?

All trainers go through an ID check, in-person interview and reference check.

Do your trainers have insurance?

All trainers are required to hold adequate civil liability insurance which covers legal liability for death, injury or illness to others and loss of, or damage to, third party property. The Register requires fitness professionals to hold an annual liability policy with a minimum indemnity of £5,000,000.

How do I become a TruBe trainer?

We're always interested in hearing from ambitious trainers. Please sign-up using the online application form and we'll take it from there.

Preparing for a session

Do I need to bring any equipment to my session?

You won't need to bring anything other than a towel and water. Our trainers are equipped with portable state-of-the art fitness equipment.

How much space do I need?

Our trainers will be able to train you in any space as larger than a yoga mat (2mx1m).

Can I train if I am injured or hurt?

You can exercise if you are injured. Please fill in all the necessary information in your profile page.

How should I prepare for my workout?

To make the most of your session time, it's best to arrive wearing your training clothes. Please think of bringing water and a towel.

How can I contact my trainer?

You'll find a mobile number listed in their profile. When you book, you'll also have a chance to add notes with any additional information or directions you think your trainer needs to know.

What happens if the weather becomes really bad just before or during my session?

Your trainer will be prepared to adapt your session to suit the weather. Unfortunately, we can't be held liable for any unforeseen weather conditions.

Our Venues

How do we choose our venues?

Each of our venues are hidden workout gems in London. From traditional dance studios to luxurious gyms, each venue has all the equipment you need.

Why is there an extra charge to workout in a venue?

The gyms and studios we have partnered with are all independently owned and a day pass must be purchased to enter and use the facilities. Each venue charge different rates.

How do I pay for the venue?

Payment will either be made to the trainer or the venue when you arrive for your session. Each venue is different, please check the individual venue page in the app to see who you will need to pay and whether they accept card or cash.

Can I train with any trainer?

Each venue will only allow access to specific trainers. These trainers will be highlighted on the individual venue page in the app.

When will TruBe come to my area?
